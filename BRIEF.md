# IslamChristianity.com — Project Brief

## Goal
Platform where Muslims and Christians can find **factual, sourced** information on important theological/historical topics.

## Core Principle
Fact-based, not preachy. Each topic covered from BOTH sides with primary sources.

## Positioning
Neutral reference library — facts from each tradition's own sources. Not debate. Not conversion. Just: "Here's what they say, here's the source."

## Topics (Priority Order)
1. **The Crucifixion** — Did Jesus die on the cross?
   - Christian: Yes (Gospels, Paul)
   - Islamic: No (Quran 4:157-158)
2. **Jesus' Nature** — Son of God or prophet?
   - Christian: Divine, second person of Trinity
   - Islamic: Messiah, born of virgin, human prophet
3. **The Quran vs Bible** — Which is preserved?
   - Christian: Bible is authoritative
   - Islamic: Quran is final, Bible corrupted (tahrif)
4. **Trinity** — Is God three-in-one?
   - Christian: Yes, orthodox doctrine
   - Islamic: No, shirk (Quran 4:171, 5:73)
5. **Salvation** — How saved?
   - Christian: Faith in Christ's sacrifice
   - Islamic: Faith + good works + Allah's mercy
6. **Muhammad** — Who is he?
   - Islamic: Final prophet, seal of prophets
   - Christian: Not recognized as prophet

## Site Structure
- `/` — Homepage
- `/topics/crucifixion/` — One page per topic
- Each topic page: Islamic position → Christian position → Sources
- Clean, minimal, mobile-first
- Primary sources quoted directly with citations
